About Us

Triple Z Tech Computer Store is built on the premise that managing information technology for businesses is not inherently a do-it-yourself project, similar to receiving legal advice, accounting, or graphic design. Triple Z Tech Computer Store aims to be a trusted ally for our clients, providing them with the loyalty of a business partner and the economics of an outside vendor. We ensure that our clients have everything they need to run their businesses as efficiently and reliably as possible. Because many of our information applications are mission-critical, we assure our clients that we will be available when they need us. Triple Z Tech Computer Store provides both computer products and services to small, medium, and large businesses. We specialize in providing hardware laptops and desktops, network systems, and services to small, medium, and large businesses. Triple Z Tech Computer Store is a computer retailer-based technology. It evolved into a retailer to meet the market demand for personal computers and now emphasizes service and support to differentiate itself from more price oriented national chains.

OUR EXPERIENCE

Triple Z Tech Computer Store has been caught in the vice grip of margin demand, which has promoted local computer retailers. We are without a doubt a consulting-oriented value-added retailer. Triple Z Tech Computer Store has distinguished itself from price-oriented competitors where our products and services have been contracted speaks volumes about us, and this is what keeps us in business.

 

OUR VALUE PROPOSITION

Our value proposition must be distinct from that of the typical box-oriented retail chain. We provide our target customer, who is looking for service and is not self-sufficient, with a vendor who acts as a strategic ally at a premium price that reflects the value of knowing that the systems will work.

 

 

COMPETITIVE EDGE

Our competitive advantage is our positioning as a strategic ally with our clients, who are more than just customers to us. We build defences against the competition by building a business on long-term relationships with satisfied clients. The longer the relationship exists, the more we assist our clients in understanding what we offer and why they need it.

 


SALES STRATEGY

We must sell the company rather than the product. We do not sell MSI, Gigabyte, Asus, TP-Link, or any of our software and hardware brand names; we only sell Triple Z. We must market our service and support. The hardware is analogous to a razor, and the razor blades are support, service, and software services. We must provide our customers with what they truly require.
